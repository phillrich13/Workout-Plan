Recommended Weekly Layout

The plan alternates strength and walking days to allow recovery. Strength days are placed on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday so your legs recover in between for walking. Saturday remains your long hike day. Sunday is full rest.

Monday Day A — Lower Body + Core
Tuesday Walk 3-4 mi
Wednesday Day B — Upper Body Push + Pull
Thursday Walk 3-4 mi
Friday Day C — Lower Power + Upper Accessories
Saturday Hike 4-8 mi
Sunday Full Rest / Light Stretch

Google Sheets Setup

To enable cross-device sync:

  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Go to Extensions > Apps Script
  3. Paste the Apps Script code (in your google_apps_script.js file)
  4. Click Deploy > New deployment > Web app
  5. Set "Execute as" = Me, "Who has access" = Anyone
  6. Copy the Web App URL and paste it below
